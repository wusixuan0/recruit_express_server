 ##  **Senior Digital Marketing Manager**

**Impact & Overview**

As our Senior Digital Marketing Manager, you will play a pivotal role in driving brand awareness, user engagement, and business growth through innovative and impactful digital marketing strategies.  You will lead the development and execution of comprehensive digital marketing campaigns across various channels, leveraging your expertise in SEO/SEM, content creation, and social media to reach new audiences and convert them into loyal customers.  Your analytical skills will be crucial in monitoring campaign performance and optimizing strategies for maximum ROI.

**Responsibilities**

* Develop and implement comprehensive digital marketing strategies across all channels (SEO, SEM, social media, content marketing, email marketing).
* Conduct thorough market research to identify target audiences, analyze competitor strategies, and identify emerging trends.
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams (product, design, sales) to align marketing campaigns with overall business objectives.
* Manage and optimize website content, ensuring it is search engine friendly and engaging for users.
* Develop and execute social media campaigns to build brand presence, drive user engagement, and generate leads.
* Track campaign performance using analytics tools, identify areas for improvement, and continually optimize strategies for maximum ROI.
* Stay up-to-date on the latest digital marketing trends and technologies to ensure our campaigns remain effective.

**Qualifications**

* Proven experience in a digital marketing role, preferably with a focus on SEO/SEM.
* Strong understanding of digital marketing principles, including content marketing, social media marketing, and email marketing.
* Expertise in content creation, including writing, editing, and visual design.
* Excellent analytical skills with the ability to interpret data and translate it into actionable insights.
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to work effectively with diverse teams.
* Self-motivated and results-oriented with a passion for digital marketing.

**Education and Experience**

* Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field.
* 5+ years of experience in digital marketing, with a proven track record of success.

**Benefits & Compensation**

* Competitive salary based on experience.
* Comprehensive health insurance coverage.
* Retirement plan with employer matching.
* Work-from-home flexibility.
* Professional development allowance.

**Company Culture**

We foster a collaborative and fast-paced environment where creativity and innovation are highly valued.  We are a team of passionate individuals who are committed to delivering exceptional service and exceeding expectations. Open communication, continuous learning, and a shared commitment to our mission are core to our company culture.

**Opportunity for Growth and Learning**

We are dedicated to supporting your professional growth through:

* On-the-job training opportunities.
* Access to industry-leading workshops and conferences.
* Mentorship programs to guide you on your career path.

**Day in the Life**

Your day-to-day activities will involve a blend of strategic thinking, creative execution, and data analysis:

* Analyze campaign data and identify key performance indicators.
* Craft compelling social media content that resonates with our target audiences.
* Collaborate with the SEO team to optimize website content for search engine visibility.
* Participate in creative brainstorming sessions to develop new marketing initiatives.

**Specific Project Descriptions**

You will have the opportunity to lead the development and launch of a new mobile application aimed at enhancing user engagement. Your responsibilities will include:

* Defining the application's target audience and key features.
* Collaborating with the development team to ensure the application meets user needs.
* Creating marketing materials and campaigns to promote the app launch.
* Monitoring user feedback and iterating on the app based on data insights.

**Mission & Vision Alignment**

We are seeking a creative visionary who is passionate about making a positive impact through our digital marketing efforts.  Your ability to translate our mission of social impact into compelling visual narratives that amplify our brand's commitment to sustainable practices will be key to your success in this role.
